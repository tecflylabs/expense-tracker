FAQ

  • How do I add a receipt photo? Open a transaction → Details → Receipts → “Add photo”.
  • How do I delete a receipt? Swipe the receipt row → Delete.
  • How do I export my data? Open Settings → Export (CSV/PDF).
  • I found a bug / have a feature request. Email us and include your iPhone model + iOS version.
