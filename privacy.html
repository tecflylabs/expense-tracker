Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-07

Overview

PennyFlow is an expense tracking app. Your data is stored locally on your device unless stated otherwise in this policy.

Data we process

  • Financial entries you create (transactions, categories, notes, tags, budgets).
  • Receipt attachments (photos) you add to transactions, stored locally on your device.
  • App settings (e.g. currency, theme, biometric lock preference) stored on-device.

Accounts

PennyFlow does not require an account.

Analytics / Tracking

PennyFlow does not use third‑party advertising tracking. If analytics/crash reporting is added in the future, this policy will be updated.

Permissions

  • Photos access: only used to select a receipt photo to attach to a transaction.
  • Biometrics (optional): used only to lock/unlock the app on your device.
