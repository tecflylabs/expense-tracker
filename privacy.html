1. Overview

PennyFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance tracking application developed by Manuel Zangl. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use PennyFlow.

Our Core Principle: Your financial data is yours alone. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal financial information.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Financial Data (Stored Locally Only)

The following data is created and stored exclusively on your device:

  • Transactions: Title, amount, date, category, type (income/expense), notes, tags
  • Budget Goals: Category limits, start dates, monthly targets
  • Recurring Transactions: Scheduled payment details and frequencies
  • Receipt Photos: Images you attach to transactions (stored in local app storage)
  • App Settings: Currency preference, theme selection, Face ID settings

Important: None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third party. It remains on your iPhone.

2.2 Data We DO NOT Collect

  • ‚ùå Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • ‚ùå Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ‚ùå Usage analytics or crash reports
  • ‚ùå Location data
  • ‚ùå Contacts or calendar access
  • ‚ùå Browsing history or app usage patterns

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Local Storage

All financial data is stored in a SwiftData database on your device's secure storage. This data is protected by iOS's sandboxing and encryption mechanisms.

3.2 iCloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable iCloud Backup in your iPhone Settings, your PennyFlow data will be included in encrypted device backups. This is controlled by Apple, not by us. You can disable this in:

Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Backup ‚Üí Disable for PennyFlow

3.3 Face ID / Touch ID

If enabled, biometric authentication is handled entirely by iOS. We do not store or have access to your biometric data. We only receive a success/failure response from the system.

3.4 Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Manually delete transactions within the app
  • Uninstall the app (which deletes all local data)
  • Reset your iPhone

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple App Store

PennyFlow is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect anonymous download statistics and crash reports. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours.

5.2 In-App Purchases (StoreKit)

If you purchase PennyFlow Pro, the transaction is processed by Apple. We use Apple's StoreKit framework to verify purchases, but we do not store your payment information or purchase history.

5.3 No Analytics or Advertising

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar tracking tools
  • Facebook SDK or social media integrations
  • Ad networks (e.g., AdMob, Meta Audience Network)
  • Crash reporting services (e.g., Crashlytics, Sentry)

6. In-App Purchases & Pro Features

PennyFlow offers a one-time Pro purchase that unlocks premium features. This purchase is:

  • Processed by Apple (not us)
  • Non-subscription (lifetime access)
  • Verified locally using StoreKit
  • Restorable on the same Apple ID across devices

We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple handles all billing and refunds.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

PennyFlow is rated 4+ (Age 4 and up) and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we collect no data from any users, the app is safe for all ages. Parents should supervise young children's use of financial apps.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & Privacy Laws)

Under privacy regulations like GDPR, CCPA, and others, you typically have rights such as:

  • Right to Access: All your data is accessible within the app at all times
  • Right to Delete: Delete transactions or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Right to Export: Pro users can export data as CSV or PDF
  • Right to Portability: Export your data and import it elsewhere

Important: Since we don't collect or store your data on our servers, these rights are automatically fulfilled by the app's local-first design.

  • Legal or regulatory changes
  • Improvements to our privacy practices

  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page

Continued use of PennyFlow after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
