Privacy Policy – PennyFlow

Last updated: 2026-01-07

Overview

PennyFlow is an expense tracking app. Your data is stored locally on your device unless stated otherwise in this policy.

Data we process

  • Financial entries you create (transactions, categories, notes, tags, budgets).
  • Attachments/receipts (photos) you add to transactions are stored locally on your device.
  • App settings (e.g. currency, theme, biometric lock preference) are stored on-device.

Data collection

PennyFlow does not require you to create an account.

By default, PennyFlow does not sell your data and does not share your personal data with third parties for advertising.

Analytics / Crash Reports

Currently, PennyFlow does not use third‑party analytics or third‑party crash reporting. If this changes, this policy will be updated.

Permissions

  • Photos access: used only to let you select a receipt photo to attach to a transaction.
  • Biometrics (optional): used only to lock/unlock the app on your device.
